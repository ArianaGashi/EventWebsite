FAQs

Is the event free?

Yes, tickets are free but registration is mandatory.

Who can attend?

Anyone interested in technology, whether you're a beginner or an expert.

What is the language of the event?

The event will be conducted in English.

Do I need to bring my laptop?

It's not required, but bringing a laptop is recommended for workshops and hands-on activities.

Will food and drinks be provided?

Yes, we will provide breakfast snacks, lunch, and refreshments throughout the day.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, there is limited parking available on a first-come, first-served basis.

How can I become a speaker at the event?

You can apply to be a speaker by contacting us at info@gdgprishtina.com.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, sessions will be recorded and shared on our website after the event.

Are there any sponsorship opportunities?

Yes, for sponsorship inquiries, please reach out to us at sponsor@gdgprishtina.com.

How do I volunteer for the event?

If you're interested in volunteering, please contact us at volunteer@gdgprishtina.com.
